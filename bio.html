Meet Hazel



Former America's Got Talent© Winner


Three BILLBOARD© number ones


Nominated for five Grammys©


A 17 year old with a passion for singing that began when she was little.


Hazel sang as a child in her elementary's choir.
At the age of ten, Hazel had already learned to play the piano and the guitar.
By twelve, Hazel had released her first youtube clip of her singing and it reached 10,000 likes.
At fourteen, Hazel auditioned and won America's Got Talent and was seen as, "the next big star".
By 15, Hazel released her first album which charted on BILLBOARD as number one for seven weeks.
At 16, Hazel had been nominated and won five Grammys.

TODAY
